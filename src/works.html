Xover Weapons

ACS / Zandronum

A mod for Mega Man 8-bit Deathmatch which adds a system to combine weapons

The combined weapon is stronger, and defined by the ingredients' traits, like element or firing style. A fusion can be used as an ingredient as well into even stronger weapons.

50+ brand new weapons coded

Unity

A 360°/ stereoscopic ("VR") video based on a Sonic stage

Rhythm/management game

As a blacksmith, forge weapons through DDR-like sequences to make a profit for yourself, or offer them to the deities.
